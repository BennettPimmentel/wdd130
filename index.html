Perú

Peru is a country in South America famous for its Incan history, Machu Picchu, and diverse geography that includes the coast, the mountains, and the jungle. Its capital is Lima. Peru is a country rich in culture and history, with a fascinating mix of ancient traditions and modern influences. The country's landscapes range from the arid coastal desert to the high peaks of the Andes mountains, offering endless opportunities for adventure and exploration. Peru is also known for its delicious cuisine, particularly dishes like ceviche and the famous Peruvian potato varieties.
