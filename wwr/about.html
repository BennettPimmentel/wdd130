History

White Water Rafting began over 20 years ago with a group of friends who shared a deep passion for adventure and the great outdoors. What started as weekend trips soon became a full-fledged company committed to sharing the thrill of whitewater rafting with others.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted outfitter known for our safety standards, experienced guides, and unforgettable river experiences. From calm family floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, our mission has always been the same: help people connect with nature through adventure.
